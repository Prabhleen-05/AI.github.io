ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

What is artificial intelligence ?

Artificial intelligence = artificial + intelligence

Artificial = Not Natural

Intelligence = The capacity to learn and solve problems

Artificial Intelligence : Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence by machines.

The ability to solve problems.
The ability to act rationally.
The ability to act like humans

EVOLUTION OF AI

There are diffrent stages in the evolution of artificial intelligence some stages are mentioned below:

1) Maturation of Artificial Intelligence (1943-1952)
2) The birth of Artificial Intelligence (1952-1956)
3) The golden years-Early enthusiasm (1956-1974)
4) The first AI winter (1974-1980)
5) The emergence of intelligent agents (1993-2011)
6) Deep learning, big data and artificial general intelligence (2011-present)
